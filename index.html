What is Thunder Engine?

Thunder Engine is a game-changer in the world of game development. It's a free and open-source multi-functional lightweight game engine that empowers creators to craft stunning 2D and 3D games effortlessly. Our mission is to simplify game development so that you can focus on what you do best - creating captivating games!

Why Thunder Engine?

Free Open Source Game Engine

Say goodbye to licensing fees and restrictions! Thunder Engine is completely free for all purposes. Whether you're a hobbyist, indie developer, or part of a game studio, you can harness the full power of Thunder Engine without breaking the bank.

Cross-Platform

Thunder Engine provides the ability to create games for various platforms, including Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android, and more. Our powerful game engine ensures that your game will work on any device.

Embedded Editor

Thunder Engine comes with an integrated editor that offers a wide range of tools for game content development. Edit graphics, fine-tune audio, and create unique worlds with ease, all in one place.
